About Us

Neuron Interface Project (NIP) develops cost-effective and novel devices for in vitro neural stimulation and recording. Our systems combine high-resolution microelectrode arrays, closed-loop control, and intuitive software interfaces for cutting-edge neuroscience research.

Technology

  • Microelectrode Arrays
    Multi-channel stimulation and recording with sub-millisecond precision.
  • Closed-Loop Stimulation
    Real-time response-adaptive protocols for plasticity research.
  • Waveform Control
    Customizable biphasic waveforms, amplitude scaling, and temporal resolution.

Applications

  • Neural circuit research
  • Pharmacological testing
  • Stem cell-derived neuron stimulation
  • Brain-computer interface prototyping
